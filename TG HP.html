Who We Are

Our squad name is Thurgood Marshall Top Twerk Team(TMTTT). Our goal this semester is to create fun, creative, and entertaining apps- similar to the art form of twerking.

Our Goals This semester

To create dope apps and run the world.

What We've Covered

MoleMash: Based off the Whac-A-Mole arcade game, this game displays moles in random places and the user will gain points when they hit the mole.

Paintpot: App that allows users to draw on an image in different colors. Users can take a photo and draw on that image instead.

HelloPurr: App with a cat button that meows when you tuch it andpurrs when you shake the device.

Angie Hoffman

Angie is the amazing teacher we're working with this year. She's taken her Game Designer toolbox and became a high school teacher with the creation of the Game Design Academy in concert with Zynga and AppInventor classes. She believes that technology should be a core part of education and as a result will diversify the industry. Therefore she has created a two year Computer Science program that teaches students to code, animate, test, market, and pitch via video games. It's the most popular class in school three years running.
