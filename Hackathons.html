What is a hackathon?

Hackathons provide a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology. People who are interested in technology come together, form teams and try to solve a problem or take an idea and turn it into a reality.

Aside from solving problems and creating apps, websites, and softwares that are out of this world, you also get free stuff! Free food, free shirts, socks, keychains, and the list goes on!

There are also workshops that teach different programming languages and teach you how to write code for different gadgets like drones, automated laser pens, and 3D printers!

But how does it all work?

Show up. Meet people. Get free stuff. Play with cool gadgets. Builds dope apps. Have the nerdiest and best time of your life. Hackthons tend to have a certain theme with prizes geared toward that theme. Read about our upcoming hackathon!
