Who we are

We are a group of students and factulty from the University of San Francisco who want to empower people of all different genders and backgrounds to explore the field of computer science by providing them with the assistance and tools necessary to succeed in tech.

Our two main programs are Girl Tech Power and CS4HS in partnership with Google.

At Girl Tech Power, we teach middle school and high school girls the basics of HTML, Python, App Inventor, and computer architecture. We feel that girls feel intimidated in the tech field because they are not what you think of when you think of a "stereotypical" programmer. We want to break that stereotype.

CS4HS is a program in which we go to high schools throughout the bay area and help teachers develop a curriculum to introduce programming at a young age. We believe that many people shy away from computer science due to a lack of exposure in high school. It is often seen as a mysterious field with impossible to read algorithms. We want to create an environment that breeds exploration and innovation in the field of computer science beginning at a young age.
