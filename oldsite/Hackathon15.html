When is it?

The Hackathon will take place on December 5th from 9:30 AM to 5:00PM. Please fill out this Google Form so we will know if you're coming or not! Deadline is November 13th.

Where is it?

The Hackathon will be hosted at the University of San Francisco which is located at 2130 Fulton St., San Francisco, CA 94117.

What will you be doing?

You are going to take everything you learned about AppInventor so far and let you explore your creativity. You can form teams with your friends or meet new people from other schools, come up with an idea for an app. Over the course of 5 hours, you and your team will create a fully functioning app. We will also have different prizes for our three main themes: Social Impact, Games, and Utility.

What if you can't think of an idea or don't know how to make an app?

Hackathons are just as much about learning as they are about creating. We will be hosting workshops for App Inventor to teach tips and tricks for those who just want to learn App Inventor and see how apps are created from start to finish.

Most importantly, will there be food?

Rule #1 of hackathons: free food. We will be providing lunch and snacks throughout the day. Programmers simply do not function without food.

Do you have more questions?

Shoot us an email at cs.usfca.edu@gmail.com.
