Who We Are

Our squad name is Thurgood Marshall Top Twerk Team(TMTTT). Our goal this semester is to create fun, creative, and entertaining apps- similar to the art form of twerking.

Our Goals This semester

To create dope apps and run the world.

What We've Covered

Creative Halloween: For this project students created apps based solely on their creativity and Halloween spirit.

MoleMash: Based off the Whac-A-Mole arcade game, this game displays moles in random places and the user will gain points when they hit the mole.

Paintpot: App that allows users to draw on an image in different colors. Users can take a photo and draw on that image instead.

Raffle: App that allows people to text the phone number of the device, collects the phone numbers from received text messages, and randomly selects and calls the winning phone number to award a prize.

HelloPurr: App with a cat button that meows when you touch it and purrs when you shake the device.

I Have a Dream: A soundboard app that displays two photos. One of Martin Luther King and one of Malcolm X. When users touch the MLK image the app plays his famous "I Have a Dream" speech. When users touch the Malcolm X image his "Our History Was Destroyed by Slavery" speech.
