What is CS4HS?

"While an introductiong to CS concepts at earlier ages encourages more diversity among scholars and practitioners in the field, there are not enough instructors prepared to guide our next generation of innovators." -Google

CS4HS is a program in which we go to high schools throughout the bay area and help teachers develop a curriculum to introduce programming at a young age. We believe that many people shy away from computer science due to a lack of exposure in high school. It is often seen as a mysterious field with impossible to read algorithms. We want to create an environment that breeds exploration and innovation in the field of computer science beginning at a young age.

What have we done so far?

We have currently reached out to five bay area high schools and are currently working with three of them. We go to the schools once to three times a week and help the teachers build a curriculum to teach the basics of App Inventor. This is our first semester working with the schools and we hope to expand to more schools around the bay area and introduce more students into the field of technology. Explore our website to learn more about the projects that we are working on with the local schools.
